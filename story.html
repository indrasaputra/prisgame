Story of PRIS Game

Cipio, a logic lover, wanted to join an amazing competition called National Logic Competition (NLC), a competition for junior and senior high school student held by Schematics, an annual event of Department of Informatics, Faculty of Information Technology, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember.
He asked for princess' permission since he has been a princess guard. The princess said that she would give him a permission if he brought purpleberry as many as possible. So, he went to Berryland to collect some purpleberries.
Unfortunately, the princess didn't want him to leave her side but she couldn't stop Cipio. So, she made a competition. She will send you to Berryland. If Cipio collects more purpleberries than you, the princess will give him a permission. Since the princess will be lonely without him, you need to collect purpleberries more than Cipio does. But, Cipio will not give up so easily.
